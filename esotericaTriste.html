Overview

Concept
A modern digital ritual to process collective and ancestral sorrow. A symbiotic hybrid installation designed to move the user from passive sadness into active lament.
02
The Installation
Two inseparable parts: a physical altar charged with melted black candles, exposed cables, and LED bars — and "THE PASSAGE," a 5-minute VR experience mapping the biological stages of death into sonic and visual states.

The Passage
A VR memory dive structured by THANATO-MORPHO — a lament song translating death's biological stages (Pallor, Algor, Livor, Rigor Mortis) into evolving sonic states.
05
Hypothesis
Contemporary culture veils death in silence. Death is not an ending but the most shared thread connecting every life across generations — a passage, not an absence.
